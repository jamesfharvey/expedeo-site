The Problem

Every year, organizations lose billions in revenue, productivity and public trust to a problem hiding in plain sight: poorly managed service flow.

No-show rates run 15 to 35% of scheduled appointments. Lobbies fill with frustrated customers who have poor visibility into their expected wait. Staff manage demand with clipboards, paper tickets or rigid legacy systems built 25+ years ago. And management is left with spreadsheets and gut feel to answer complex questions like how to staff next Tuesday to meet demand.

Customers today track their rideshare in real time and get a text when their table is ready. Then they walk into a clinic or government office and take a paper ticket.

Existing solutions were designed for a different era. Often on-premise, inflexible, built to digitize the paper ticket, not to optimize operations. They manage lines. They don't improve them.

The Solution

Expedeo doesn't just manage your lines and appointments. It helps you run a better operation.

Purpose-built for higher education, state and local governments and healthcare. Industries where every visit matters and every minute counts.

Increase capacity without increasing headcount.

Expedeo coordinates walk-ins, appointments, virtual visits and callbacks into a single intelligent workflow, giving managers the insight to put the right employees on the right services at the right time. Less idle time, faster service and more efficient operations.

Real-time operational intelligence.

Expedeo doesn't just show you what's happening on your service floor, it tells you what to do about it. The system detects bottlenecks, flags service delays and delivers specific recommendations so managers can act before small problems become big ones. No digging through dashboards. No waiting for end-of-week reports. Expedeo surfaces what matters, when it matters.

Create an experience customers notice.

Customers can check in at a kiosk, through reception or from their phone before they arrive, however your operations work best. Customers receive real-time updates on their place in line and show up when it's nearly their turn, not 45 minutes early to sit in a crowded lobby. Shorter perceived waits and smoother visits lead to higher satisfaction scores and Google Reviews.

Happier customers, less stressed staff.

When lobbies aren't packed and service flows smoothly, frontline employees aren't constantly managing frustrated customers. Better customer experiences create better working conditions, and better working conditions create better customer experiences.

Intelligent by design. Controlled by you.

Expedeo comes with built-in intelligence and automation designed to protect your operations, from surge detection and bottleneck alerts to intake throttling and smart routing for at-risk customers. Every capability is configurable. You decide what to turn on, what to automate and what stays manual. The intelligence is there when you want it, and always under your control.
